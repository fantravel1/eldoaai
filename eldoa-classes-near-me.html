Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find ELDOA classes near me?
Use the ELDOA Practitioners Directory and click "Find Near Me" to locate certified Level 4+ instructors who offer group classes in your area. You can filter by country or search by city name.
What happens in an ELDOA class?
ELDOA classes typically last 45-60 minutes and include guided spinal decompression postures, fascial stretching, proprioceptive exercises, and breathing techniques. Instructors help you maintain proper form for each position.
How much do ELDOA classes cost?
Group classes typically range from $20-50 per session. Many studios offer class packages at discounted rates, and some instructors provide introductory offers for new students.
Do I need experience to join an ELDOA class?
No prior experience is needed. Certified instructors guide you through each posture and provide modifications based on your fitness level. Many studios offer beginner-friendly intro classes.
Are there online ELDOA classes available?
Yes! Many certified instructors offer virtual classes. Check our practitioners directory for online options, or explore our Video Library for instructional content.
What should I bring to class?
Bring a yoga mat, comfortable clothing, and water. Some classes use props like blocks or straps - check with your instructor. Arrive 10-15 minutes early for your first class.
How often should I attend ELDOA classes?
For best results, attend 2-3 classes per week. Daily home practice between classes, even just 10-15 minutes, will accelerate your progress and spinal health benefits.
