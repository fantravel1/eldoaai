Detailed Explanation (Encyclopedia)

Active fascial tension distinguishes ELDOA from passive therapies. Rather than relying on external traction or simple stretching, practitioners consciously engage muscles along specific myofascial chains to generate precise tension patterns. This engagement creates space within joints and along the spine while stimulating mechanotransduction pathways that passive approaches cannot reach.
