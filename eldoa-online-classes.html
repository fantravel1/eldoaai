Frequently Asked Questions

Can I take ELDOA classes online?
Yes! Many certified ELDOA instructors offer online classes via Zoom. Popular options include H3TV (subscription library), ELDOA METHOD NZ (live + on-demand), Toronto ELDOA (live Mon/Thu/Sun), and ELDOAUSA (live group classes).
How much do online ELDOA classes cost?
Pricing varies by provider. H3TV and ELDOA METHOD offer subscription models for unlimited access. Toronto ELDOA and Twilight Stretching charge per class. Many providers offer introductory rates for new students.
What equipment do I need?
You need a yoga mat, comfortable clothing, and floor space (approximately 6x8 feet). A computer, tablet, or phone with Zoom is required for live sessions. Yoga blocks and straps are optional but helpful.
Are online classes effective?
Online ELDOA classes can be very effective from certified Level 4+ instructors who provide clear cues and demonstrations. Many students successfully learn and maintain their practice virtually. For initial learning or specific corrections, consider at least a few in-person sessions if possible.
What time zones do live classes accommodate?
Different providers accommodate different time zones. Toronto ELDOA serves North American times (EST), ELDOA METHOD NZ offers sessions for Asia-Pacific, and providers in Europe offer local times. Check each provider for their specific schedule.
