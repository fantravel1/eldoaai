About Our Research

ELDOA (Étirements Longitudinaux avec Decoaptation Ostéo-Articulaire) is backed by decades of clinical practice and emerging scientific evidence. Our research articles provide in-depth analysis of the neurological, biomechanical, and physiological mechanisms that make ELDOA effective for spinal health, athletic performance, and overall wellness.

Each article is based on Guy Voyer's original work, current scientific literature, and clinical observations from practitioners worldwide. We bridge the gap between traditional practice and modern evidence-based medicine.
