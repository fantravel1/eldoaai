Submission Guidelines

To ensure the quality and integrity of ELDOA AI, all contributions must meet the following standards:

Quality Standards

  • Accuracy: All information must be factually correct and aligned with ELDOA methodology as taught by Dr. Guy Voyer
  • Evidence-Based: Content should be grounded in research, clinical experience, and proper ELDOA training
  • Clear and Accessible: Write or present in a way that's understandable to both practitioners and general audiences
  • Proper Attribution: Give credit where due ‚Äî cite sources, reference Dr. Voyer's work, and acknowledge contributors

Rights and Permissions

  • You must own or have permission to share any content you submit
  • By submitting content, you grant ELDOA AI a non-exclusive license to use, display, and distribute it on the platform
  • You will be credited as the contributor (unless you request anonymity)
  • All submissions are subject to review and editorial approval

Review Process

All contributions go through a review process to ensure quality, accuracy, and alignment with ELDOA principles:

  1. Submission: Email your contribution to contact@eldoaai.com
  2. Review: Our team reviews content for accuracy, quality, and suitability
  3. Feedback: We may reach out with questions, requests for revisions, or clarifications
  4. Publication: Once approved, your contribution will be published with proper attribution
